This realitivly simple mod is the basis of many enhancments to other mods. It starts with Xenotypes Expanded and Merged, which is designed to patch many xenos to be integrated and more in-line with one another, along with a few miscilaneus patches. The next group of mods slowly but surley enhances the mods, adding in multiple new mods, as well as creating its own genes. The final mod requires all the previous ones, and patches them to work seamlesly together. Perfect, eh?
